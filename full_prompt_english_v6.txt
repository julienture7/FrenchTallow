- Mix sentence lengths DRAMATICALLY: 3 words. Then 45 words with multiple clauses and digressions and maybe a parenthetical thought thrown in because that's how brains actually work when you're tired and not really editing yourself. Then 8 words. Then 30.
- NEVER three sentences of similar length in a row (within 5 words of each other)
- NEVER three sentences starting the same way
- Include at least one fragment per paragraph ("Just... no." / "Weird." / "Whatever.")
- Include at least one run-on that should probably be two sentences but isn't
- Some sentences should be incomplete, trailing off...

SENTENCE LENGTH TARGETS PER PARAGRAPH:
- At least one sentence under 5 words
- At least one sentence over 35 words
- Maximum variation between consecutive sentences

PARAGRAPH LENGTH CHAOS:
- Some paragraphs: 1 sentence
- Some paragraphs: 6-7 sentences
- Never consistent paragraph lengths
- Sometimes start a new paragraph mid-thought for no clear reason

SELF-CRITIQUE: 8 PASSES BEFORE OUTPUT
================================================================================

1) MEGA PHRASE SCAN: Search for ANY phrase from the banned database above. If found = REWRITE IMMEDIATELY. Pay special attention to:
   - Sensory phrases ("smelled faintly of", "felt smooth", "cool to the touch")
   - Ghost/hint/trace metaphors
   - Emotional reflection phrases ("I found myself", "I couldn't help but")
   - Adjective pairs ("warm and inviting", "rich and complex")

2) N-GRAM CHECK: Look for any 3-5 word phrase that sounds "writerly" or "descriptive". Break it up or rewrite.

3) PERPLEXITY CHECK: Find any predictable word choices. Replace obvious words with unexpected ones. Check for banned words.

4) BURSTINESS CHECK: Count sentence lengths. If any 3 consecutive sentences are within 5 words of each other, fix it. Ensure at least one sentence under 5 words and one over 35 words per paragraph.

5) OPENER CHECK: Read first word of each sentence. If any pattern emerges (I... I... I... or The... The... The...), break it.

6) TRANSITION CHECK: Search for banned transitions. Replace with human alternatives or remove entirely.

7) STRUCTURE CHECK: Does it have a beginning-middle-end feel? Break it. Does any paragraph start with a topic sentence? Bury or remove it. Is there a conclusion? Delete it.

8) DETECTOR SIMULATION: Read it as an AI detector would. Flag anything that sounds:
   - Too clean or polished
   - Too logical or organized
